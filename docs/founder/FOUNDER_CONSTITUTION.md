# Founder Constitution (R113)

**Status:** Canonical. Repository is the source of truth — no hidden memory.

Governs the three registries and the extension order for R114 – R130. Any conflict between a conversation and this document is resolved in favour of this document.

---

## 1. Three-registry rule

HAPPY X is tracked by three registries. Each has a distinct owner and update cadence.

| Registry | Location | Owner | Update |
|---|---|---|---|
| Founder Registry | `docs/founder/FOUNDER_REGISTRY.md` | Founder / Business | Manual, never auto-generated |
| Technical Registry | `docs/technical/` | Engineering | Auto-scanned from repository |
| Feature Registry | `docs/features/` | Product | Bridges Founder + Technical |

The Founder Registry lists the 502 Founder Modules. It is the business source of truth and MUST NOT be regenerated by tooling.

## 2. Extension order (R114 – R130)

Extension work follows the sequence in `docs/IMPLEMENTATION_ROADMAP.md`. No revision may be skipped; no later revision may begin while an earlier one is Red.

## 3. Quality gates

Every change must satisfy:

1. **Green gates** — Build + Typecheck + Tests (see [`../R183_MIGRATION_GUARDRAILS.md`](../R183_MIGRATION_GUARDRAILS.md)).
2. **Extension statement** — *"Extends `<owner>` — no duplication."*
3. **No fabrication** — unknown values written as `TODO`.
4. **No hidden memory** — every permanent rule must exist in `docs/`.

## 4. Governance actions requiring Founder approval

- Adding a Founder Module to the Founder Registry.
- Retiring or renaming a Founder Module.
- Introducing a new canonical owner in the Architecture Lock.
- Deviating from the R183 guardrails.
- Publishing or submitting any store asset.

## 5. Document generation policy

All Founder-requested files, documents, and assets follow [`FOUNDER_DOCUMENT_GENERATION_POLICY.md`](./FOUNDER_DOCUMENT_GENERATION_POLICY.md). No parallel document engine.
