# HAPPY Ultimate User Experience Platform v5.0

Additive UX layer on top of the frozen Executive Dark + Aurora Dynamic
visual system. No schema, service, or business-logic changes.

## Live Island (Phase 1)
Floating top status bar surfacing thinking, listening, speaking, generating,
uploading, deploying, researching, notifications, voice, credits, network
and realtime. Click expands into the AI Activity Center.

## Universal AI Dock (Phase 2)
Glass, magnetic, magnifying dock with Home, Chat, Digital Human, Builder,
Marketplace, Notifications, Search, Settings, Founder. Auto-hide supported.

## Global Command Center (Phase 3)
CTRL+K overlay with natural-language commands, voice, quick navigation,
recent history, favourites and pinned commands.

## Global Search (Phase 4)
Users, companies, orders, documents, chats, AI memory, websites, apps,
templates, plugins, skills, files, products, invoices, notifications.

## Multi-Window Workspace (Phase 5)
Multiple workspaces, split screen, resizable panels, floating windows,
snapshots, restore, tabs.

## Picture-in-Picture (Phase 6)
Digital Human always available as a small, movable, resizable, voice-ready,
pinnable floating assistant.

## Focus & Zen (Phases 7–8)
Focus Mode: minimal interface, Pomodoro, session analytics.
Zen Mode: nature, aurora, rain, forest, ocean, night, library, coffee shop
with ambient soundscapes.

## Ambient Experience (Phase 9)
Premium UI sound layer: click, success, error, notification, voice arrival,
glass interaction, per-theme sound packs.

## AI Mood Engine (Phase 10)
Time-of-day, working-hour and context detection drives greeting, lighting,
wallpaper, theme and suggestions.

## Smart Home (Phase 11)
Dynamic dashboard: continue working, recent AI chats, recommended actions,
suggested projects, pinned workspace, recent documents.

## Workspace Templates (Phase 12)
Business, Education, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Research, Creator,
Developer, Founder, Government, Marketplace.

## Timeline (Phase 13)
Auto-save, history, snapshots, restore points, version timeline.

## Live Collaboration (Phase 14)
Realtime cursor, live presence, activity feed, comments, mentions, shared
sessions.

## AI Coach (Phase 15)
Contextual tips, interactive tutorials, learning mode, recommendations,
workflow guidance.

## Digital Human Life (Phase 16)
Idle behaviour, natural eye contact, smile, micro-expressions, thinking,
listening, waiting, celebration, empathy, context reactions.

## Gestures, Onboarding, Achievements, Streaks (Phases 17–20)
Touch / trackpad / mouse / keyboard gestures. Interactive tour + progress
tracking. XP, levels, badges, milestones, rewards. Daily / learning /
business / creator / research / founder streaks.

## Founder Experience (Phase 21)
Founder prestige theme, animation pack, dashboard effects, signature UI.

## Cursor, Icons, Widgets (Phases 22–24)
Premium cursor system (glow, trail, magnetic, contextual). Icon
marketplace (premium, business, minimal, 3D, animated). Widget platform
across productivity, market, life, founder and AI categories.

## Performance & accessibility
GPU rendering, React memo, streaming, virtualization, zero CLS, lazy
loading, code splitting, 60 fps. WCAG AAA: reduced motion, high contrast,
large text, keyboard navigation, ARIA, voice navigation, screen readers.
