# R71.2 — HAPPY Hyper Realism & Cinematic Intelligence Engine

Expansion-only. Sits on top of R71 (CLDHE) and R71.1 (Cinematic Presence).
No changes to database, RBAC, RLS, credits, wallet, pricing, notifications,
authentication, Founder AI, Presence Engine, or Universal AI Runtime.

## Pipeline

```
HappyStage
 ├─ MicroHumanLayer      (blink / breath / weight / finger jitter)
 ├─ EyeContactLayer      (cursor · focused element · speaker · camera)
 ├─ AmbientEnvLayer      (room light sampling · shadow · reflection)
 ├─ CameraIntelligence   (dolly · focus pull · zoom · scene presets)
 ├─ PresenceZoneLayer    (per-page entry / stop / greeting positions)
 ├─ FootstepSystem       (ripple · dust · reflection · adaptive audio)
 ├─ ClothSimLayer        (shoulder · sleeve · hair micro-motion)
 └─ MoodEngine           (auto-selects emotion from context)
```

Every layer honors the active quality tier from R71.1 and
`prefers-reduced-motion`.

## Micro human engine

Deterministic pseudo-random schedules keyed off `performance.now()` so
motion never falls into a visible loop:
- blink: mean 3.6 s, jitter ±1.4 s, occasional double-blink
- micro-smile: mean 12 s, amplitude 0.05–0.12
- head tilt: bounded ±3°
- shoulder shift: bounded ±1.5 px, phase-shifted from head
- finger relax: independent 8–14 s cycle
- weight shift: idle balance every 6–9 s

## Advanced eye contact

Priority stack (top wins):
1. Speaking user (mic active)
2. Focused element (`document.activeElement` bounding box)
3. Cursor position (throttled 60 ms)
4. Current workspace hero region
5. Camera direction (fallback)

Every 4–8 s HAPPY breaks eye contact briefly (200–400 ms) toward a nearby
neutral target — never a continuous stare.

## Presence zones

Per-route entry / stop / greeting geometry. Landing, Builder, Pricing,
CRM, ERP, Marketplace, Learning, Dashboard, Founder, Support, Analytics
all get their own natural entry point and safe stop distance.

## Camera intelligence

Scene presets: `conversation`, `presentation`, `builder`, `founder`,
`meeting`. Each preset defines dolly range, focal length, DoF, and focus
subject. Camera motion is capped and disabled under reduced-motion.

## Mood engine

Auto-selects one of: professional · friendly · focused · supportive ·
celebration · concern · learning · presentation. Derived from route,
recent FAIOS command status, and last user utterance emotion.

## Performance

- All schedules are pure functions of time (no allocations per frame).
- Per-tier caps identical to R71.1.
- Cloth sim is a 2-bone verlet on shoulder + sleeve only; disabled below
  `high` tier.

## Accessibility

`prefers-reduced-motion` disables camera motion, cloth sim, footstep
particles, and dynamic lighting sweeps. Eye contact + blink stay on
because they are legibility cues.
